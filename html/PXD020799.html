Dataset Summary
TitleLarge bidirectional remodeling of the Myc-induced cell surface proteome in B-cells and prostate cells creates new opportunities for immunotherapy
DescriptionTo identify how MYC overexpression remodels the cell surface proteome in a cell autologous fashion and in different cell types, we investigated the impact of MYC overexpression on ~800 surface proteins in three isogenic model cell lines either of B-cell or prostate cell origin that were engineered to contain either high or low MYC.
