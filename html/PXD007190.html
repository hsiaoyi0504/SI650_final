Dataset Summary
TitleSWATH-MS proteomic analysis of nitrogen deficiency response of Arabidopsis
DescriptionProtein profiling of Arabidopsis thaliana seedling under nitrogen depreciation. Seven days old seedlings of Aradidopsis thaliana were transferred to nitrogen depreciated MS medium for further 48 hours cultivation. Same number of seedlings received same treatment except using MS medium were acted as control. After treatment, whole seedlings were extracted for protein profiling study.
