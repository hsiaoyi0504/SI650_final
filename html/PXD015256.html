Dataset Summary
TitlePhosphoproteomics of Mycobacterium tuberculosis DosR, DosS, and DosT
DescriptionMass Spectrometry based Phosphoproteomics was carried out on M. tuberculosis wild type strain H37Rv and the isogenic mutant strains MtbΔdosR, MtbΔdosS, and MtbΔdosT that were cultured in aerobic as well as hypoxic conditions. During later the oxygen was gradually deprived in cultures that were kept static.
