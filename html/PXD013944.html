Title: Integration of genomic, transcriptomic and proteomic insights into evolved dependence of deep-sea tubeworm holobiont
DescriptionThe first integrated genomic, transcriptomic and proteomic analysis of this methane-seep tubeworm decipher the interdependency between the host and symbionts, with an aim to reveal the molecular mechanisms enabling this worm to survive and thrive in chemosynthetic habitats.
