Dataset Summary
TitleComparative gene expression profiling of mutants of Amycolatopsis mediterranei S699
DescriptionThe project aims to unveil the mechanism of rifamycin and its analog production by wild type A. mediterranei S699 and mutant A. mediterranei DCO#34 known to produce 24-desmethylrifamycin B at two time points. Stain that donot produce this molecule is taken as the negative control in the study.
