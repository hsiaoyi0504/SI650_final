Dataset Summary
TitleOLFM4 expression in breast tumor samples
DescriptionHere we describe the first study which used SWATH MS as a tool for robust identification and quantification of breast tissus proteins. By employing a spectral library containing 3200 proteins, we show that this strategy allows the quantification of OLFM4 in each sample together with a deep proteomic coverage which provide a more comprehensive idea of the OLFM4 function in the breast cancer.
