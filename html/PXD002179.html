Dataset Summary
TitleCN-CHPP 2015 Testis Project
DescriptionThe testis tissue-based trans-omics was a feasible approach to find more missing proteins (MPs) because it was reported higher gene expression found in testis tissues. Three testis tissues and two protein separation strategies were selected to quantitatively evaluate gene expression abundance at the proteomic and transcriptomic levels. The project have been jointly supervised by Ping Xu and Siqi Liu.
