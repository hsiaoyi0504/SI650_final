Dataset Summary
TitleNCI60 proteome by PCT-SWATH - Quantitative Proteome Landscape of the NCI-60 Cancer Cell Lines
DescriptionWe obtained quantitative analysis of the NCI-60 cell panels using pressure cycling technology (PCT) and SWATH-MS. Each cell was analyzed in duplicate. The data were analyzed using a cell line SWATH assay library and OpenSWATH, followed by SWATH-expert refinement.
