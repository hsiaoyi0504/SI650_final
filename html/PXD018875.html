Dataset Summary
TitleCdh1 (Fzr1) Interactome in Neuroblastoma (N2A) cells
DescriptionIn this study, we wanted to explore the protein interactors of Cdh1, a subunit of the E3 Ubiquitin-Ligase Cdh1-APC. Our data show that in addition to interacting with other components of the Cdh1-APC complex, Cdh1 interacts with regulators of translation.
