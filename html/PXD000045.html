Dataset Summary
TitleLC-MS/MS of cultured Mycobacterium tuberculosis in a hypoxia/reaeration timecourse
DescriptionMycobacterium tuberculosis samples were collected during oxygen starvation and reaeration. The samples were solubized with urea, digested with trypsin, then desalted with a C18 96-well plate prior to LC-MS/MS analysis.
