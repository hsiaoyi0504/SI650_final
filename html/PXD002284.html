Dataset Summary
TitleNew nodes and edges in the glucosinolate molecular network revealed by proteomics and metabolomics of Arabidopsis myb28/29 and cyp79B2/B3
DescriptionIn this project we aimed to investigate new connections (edges) in the glucosinolate metabolism molecular network. We studied proteome changes in myb28/29 and cyp79B2/B3 Arabidopsis thaliana mutants as an effect of glucosinolate metabolism perturbation.
