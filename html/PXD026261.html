Dataset Summary
TitleIn vivo phosphorylation of TOC1
DescriptionMyc-tagged N-terminal portion of TOC1 or TOC1 mutant (S194A, S201A, T204A) were expressed in tobacco leaves and then immunoprecipitated. The immunoprecipitated samples were separated on SDS-PAGE gels, and then phosphorylation sites of TOC1 were identified from gel bands.
