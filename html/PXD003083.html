Dataset Summary
TitleProteomic analysis of seed germination of wheat (Triticum aestivum L.)
DescriptionIn this study, we performed the first dynamic proteome analysis of wheat seed germination using a two-dimensional differential gel electrophoresis (2D-DIGE)-based proteomic approach. A total of 166 differentially expressed protein (DEP) spots representing 73 unique proteins were identified.
