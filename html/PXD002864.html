Dataset Summary
TitleComparative proteomics of defense response to AAL toxin
DescriptionA comparative proteomics analysis on AAL toxin treated jai1 mutant and its wild type revealed that JA pathway promoted AAL toxin induced PCD by regulating the relevant PCD components and/or through other hormone pathways in a SlCOI1 dependent manner.

Additional contact: Min Zhang, Zhejiang University.
