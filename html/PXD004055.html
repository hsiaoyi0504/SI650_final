Dataset Summary
TitleLarge-scale phosphorylation site interaction proteomics
DescriptionReceptor tyrosine kinases (RTKs) are membrane proteins that regulate complex, multilayered signaling networks centered on tyrosine phosphorylation of proteins with broad implications in human health and disease. Here we developed a large-scale mass spectrometry-based proteomics platform for investigation of in-vivo RTK signaling networks and the dynamic protein interactions they induce.
