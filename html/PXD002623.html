Dataset Summary
TitleComparative shotgun proteomics of aqueous humor for cataract, glaucoma and pseudoexfoliation eye disorders.
DescriptionThe aim of this work was to characterize proteome of aqueous humor from subjects with various eye conditions such as cataract, glaucoma and pseudoexfoliation syndrome by high-resolution chromate-mass-spectrometry. Twenty nine human aqueous humor samples were processed by shotgun proteomics. Data was searched using MaxQuant package. Totally, 263 protein groups were identified. Label-free quantitation reported some differentially expressed proteins in aqueous humor proteome for the aforementioned eye diseases.
