Dataset Summary
TitleEnvironmental Pollutant Induced Cellular Injury is Reflected in Exosomes from Placental Explants
DescriptionEnvironmental exposure of placental explants did not change the quantity of exosomes or their characteristics. However, exosome cargo composition was changed by specific pollutant to reflect a biochemical signature suggestive of placental nuclear and cellular injury and inflammation.
