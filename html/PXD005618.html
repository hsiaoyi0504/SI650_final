Dataset Summary
TitleC elegans Proteomics
DescriptionReactive Oxygen Species increase gradually with aging and Steadily diminish the cell's ability to maintain homeostasis. Nuclear Factor-like 2 and its C elegans ortholog, SKN-1 are transcription factors that play a pivotal role in oxidative stress response, cellular homeostasis and lifespan. But like other defence systems, Nrf2-mediated stress response is compromised in aging and neurodegenerative diseases. In this study we provide evidence that an FDA-approved drug is a bona fide activator of Nrf2/SKN-1 pathway.
