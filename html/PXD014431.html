Dataset Summary
TitleiTRAQ-based Proteomic Analyses of the Plant-pathogenic Bacterium Acidovorax citrulli during the Viable but Nonculturable State and Resuscitation Process
DescriptionAcidovorax citrulli causes bacterial fruit blotch (BFB) disease in cucurbit crops including watermelon and melon. This bacterium can enter the viable but nonculturable (VBNC) state following exposure to copper sulfate. Moreover, copper-induced VBNC A. citrulli cells could be resuscitated by EDTA. In this study, isobaric tag for relative and absolute quantification (iTRAQ) was used to compare protein profiles of VBNC cells, resuscitated cells at different stages and log-phase cells of the A. citrulli model strain AAC00-1.
