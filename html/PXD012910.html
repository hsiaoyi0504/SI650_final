Dataset Summary
TitleMass spectrometry data of human urine
DescriptionBased on UPLC-Q-TOF-MS metabolomics technology, urine samples of 1072 subjects from 9 centers, including normal control, phlegm and blood stasis (PBS) syndrome and Qi and Yin deficiency (QYD) syndrome, and other syndromes of CHD, were conducted to find biomarkers. Among them, the discovery set (n = 125) and the test set (n = 337) were used to identify and validate biomarkers, and the validation set (n = 610) was used for the application and evaluation of the support vector machine (SVM) prediction model.
