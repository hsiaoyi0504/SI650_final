Dataset Summary
TitleExtracellular Vesicles released by Leishmania donovani infected mouse macrophages
DescriptionExtracellular vesicles (EVs) released by Leishmania donovani infected RAW264.7 murine macrophages were evaluated for their proteomic composition and potential function in pathogenesis. EVs were collected after 72 hours infection and analyzed by LC-MS/MS using both in gel and in solution digestion approaches to identify protein composition of both host and parasite origin.
