Dataset Summary
TitleH.pylori, N.gonorrhoeae, E.coli LC-MS/MS
DescriptionWe have developed approach and algorithm to annotated mass-spectra database creation and further spectral based amino acid substitution search. Algorithm is based on a robust procedure of spectral angle comparison. We have shown applicability of the algorithm to large scale datasets of Helicobacter pylori, Neisseria gonorrhoeae and Escherichia coli
