Dataset Summary
TitlenextPP for chromosome-based proteomic research using neXtProt and GENCODE databases
DescriptionIn this study, we introduce a streamlined pipeline using customized and concatenated GENCODE and neXtProt databases with controlled false discovery rate (FDR) for finding GENCODE ASVs and missing proteins while mapping onto single amino acid variants (SAAVs) from Hippocampal dataset.
