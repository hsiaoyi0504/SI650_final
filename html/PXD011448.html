Dataset Summary
TitleAshraf_Histones_characterization_P108_VS3_LTQ
DescriptionThis submission contains the mass spectrometry files for the manuscript by Kanwal Ashraf et al. that describes the functional characterization of the tetrahymena H2A, H2B, Hv1, Parp1 and Spt16. AP-MS experiments were performed from tetrahymena thermophila cells and MS files were acquired on Orbitrap Fusion and LTQ mass spectrometers. Please refer to the individual Tables 1 for additional details of submitted files. For questions, please contact Jean-Philippe Lambert (Jean-Philippe.Lambert@crchudequebec.ulaval.ca).
