Dataset Summary
TitleRapid and Deep Proteomes by Faster Sequencing on a Benchtop Quadrupole Ultra-High-Field Orbitrap Mass Spectrometer
DescriptionThe faster sequencing speed available on the latest Q Exactive HF mass spectrometer is investigated and evaluated by four different acquisition methods and benchmarked across three generations of Q Exactive instruments. Also the instrument capabilities for offline high pH reversed-phase peptide fractionation and single-shot phosphoproteomics are evaluated.
