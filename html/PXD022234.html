Dataset Summary
TitleHuman cerebrospinal fluid analysis by data-independent analysis The Cerebrospinal fluid samples were denaturated and reduced.
DescriptionCerebrospinal fluid samples of patients with Parkinson’s disease and healthy controls were used in this study. After tryptic digestion, all samples were spiked with indexed retention time (iRT) peptides and were measured using a DIA mass spectrometry approach.
