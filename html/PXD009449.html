Dataset Summary
TitleSystematic characterization of 21 post-translational modification using synthetic peptides
DescriptionThe data presented in this study in the - context of the ProteoemTools project - is based on the synthesis of about 5000 synthetic peptides carrying 21 different post-translational modifications to systematically characterize their chromatographic and mass spectrometric properties using multimodal LC-MS/MS.
