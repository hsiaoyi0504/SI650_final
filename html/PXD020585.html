Dataset Summary
Titlehuman breast cancer cell line LC-MS/MS
DescriptionTriple-negative breast cancer (TNBC) is the most aggressive type with poor prognosis among the breast cancers and has a high population of cancer stem cells (CSCs) which are main target to cure and inhibit TNBC. In this study, we analyzed the CSC-related proteome alteration by NEDD4 knockdown in CSC-abundant MDA-MB-231 cells by LC-MS/MS analysis.
