Dataset Summary
TitleSILAC APMS analysis of Pih1D3 interactome
DescriptionTo get insights into the function of Pih1D3 protein, we characterized its partners by performing a proteomic analysis in human cells. We fused this domain to GFP and stably expressed it in HeLa cells using site-specific integration with the Flp-In system. Following differential labeling of GFP-Pih1D3 and control cells with isotopically labelled amino-acids (SILAC), whole cell extracts were immuno-precipitated (IP) with anti-GFP antibodies and immunoprecipitates were subjected to quantitative mass-spectrometry analysis. SILAC labels: K0R0 control Hela H9 vs K4R6 x-FLAG- GFP- Pih1D3
