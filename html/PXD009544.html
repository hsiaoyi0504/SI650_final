Dataset Summary
TitleMolecular atlas of postnatal mouse heart development
DescriptionShotgun proteomics analysis of heart left ventricles from mice of ages 1, 4, 9, and 23 days (P01, P04, P09, and P23, respectively). The data set contains two experiments, first with P01, P04, and P09, and second with P01, P04, P09, and P23.
