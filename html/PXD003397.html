Dataset Summary
TitleGel-Free/Label-Free Proteomic Analysis Uncovers the Roles of Endoplasmic Reticulum of Soybean Root Tip under Flooding and Drought Stresses
DescriptionAs a stresses sensitive crop, this study will give some clues to enhance soybean tolerance of flooding and drought stresses. To uncover the response mechanisms occurring in the ER triggered by flooding and drought, ER proteomics was performed using gel-free/label-free proteomic approach. Besides, glycoproteomics was also producted to investigate the ER funtion in glycoprotein synthesis under stresses
