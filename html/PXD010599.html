Dataset Summary
TitleProteomic analysis of Boleophthalmus pectinirostris skin mucus
Description93,914 unigenes were sequenced from B. pectinirostris skin, providing a transcriptomic data for mucus proteomic analysis. Using Q-EXACTIVE mass spectrometer, 20366 spectra were obtained and 559 proteins were identified with 1212 matched peptides and FDR<0.01
