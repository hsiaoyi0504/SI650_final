Dataset Summary
TitleMouse retina proteomics
DescriptionProteomic experiments were performed on the outer segment (OS) and the remaining retina (RR). For the OS proteomics, three biological replicates were performed for mouse at the age of P15, which included 24, 26 and 26 retinas respectively. We also performed the OS proteomic experiment on 40 retinas from mice at age P13. For the RR proteomic experiments, two biological replicates were performed independently using 30 and 30 retinas respectively.
