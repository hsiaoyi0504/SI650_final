TitleEnvironment-driven changes of mRNA and protein levels in Pseudomonas aeruginosa
DescriptionLFQ proteome datasets of Pseudomonas aeruginosa type strain PA14 cultured at different enviromental conditions: Exponential-, transition- and stationary(12 h and 24 h)growth phases. Biofilm (24 h and 48 h), low osmolarity conditions (control = exponential phase), iron starvation and respective control, Sub-MIC ciproflox treatment and respective control.
