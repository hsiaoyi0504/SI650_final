TitleIdentification of interactors of AtNRG1B after treatment with Pfo carrying avrRps4 effector
DescriptionNRG1.2 is a helper NLR required for RRS1 RPS4 mediated immunity. RPS4 recognizes effector avrRps4 from pathogen P. syringae leading to hypersensitive response and immunity. here we co-immunoprecipitate NRG1.2-HF to identify interacting proteins upon effector recognition
