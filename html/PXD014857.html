Dataset Summary
TitleThe Drosophila CoREST interactome
DescriptionAffinity purification of dCoREST-interacting proteins from Drosophila melanogaster S2 cell nuclear extracts. An antibody recognizing both dCoREST isoforms was used to affinity purify proteins interacting with endogenous dCoREST from S2 nuclear extract. In addition, nuclear extracts from S2 cell lines ectopically expressing the FLAG-tagged dCoREST-L isoform and the FLAG-tagged dCoREST-M isoform, respectively, were subjected to anti-FLAG affinity purification to purify isoform specific dCoREST-interacting proteins.
