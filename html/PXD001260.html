Dataset Summary
TitleIdentification of miR-145 targets using SILAC
DescriptionmiR-145 is downregulated in multiple cancers. The introduction of miR-145 could alleviate the tumor burden in the pancreatic cancer mouse model. However, how miR-145 mediates the tumor suppression is still an open question. In this study, we aimed to identify the targets of miR-145 using a SILAC approach.
