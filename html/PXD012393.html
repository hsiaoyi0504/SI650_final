Dataset Summary
TitleProteogenomic characterization analysis of human large cell lung cancer
DescriptionCurrent clinical therapy of non-small cell lung cancer depends on histo-pathological classification. This approach poorly predicts clinical outcome for individual patients. Proteogenomic characterization analysis holds promise to improve clinical stratification, thus paving the way for individualized therapy. We investigated proteogenomic characterization and performed comprehensive integrative genomic analysis of human large cell lung cancer. Here we analyzed proteomes of 29 paired normal lung tissues and large cell lung cancer, identified significantly deregulated proteins associated with large cell lung cancer.
