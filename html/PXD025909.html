Dataset Summary
Titlesoybean seeds proteomics analysis of parents and recombinant inbred lines
DescriptionSoybean (Glycine max) is one of the most important economic crops. The protein and oil content traits in its seeds usually show a negative correlation, which is worthy of further study. Compared mature seeds of the high-protein soybean Tokachi long-leaf and high-oil soybean Zhonghuang 35, as well as the recombinant inbred lines of high-protein/low-oil populations and high-oil/ low-protein populations by proteomic analysis, it was found that there is a good repeatability and clear separation of proteomes with protein or oil content traits
