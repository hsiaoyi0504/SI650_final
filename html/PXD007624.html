Dataset Summary
TitleHuman aqueous humor of Primary open angle glaucoma LC-MS/MS
DescriptionThe main objective of this study was to adopt discovery based approach and define quantitative expression of human AH samples of glaucoma (N= 5) and non-glaucoma subjects (N=5) by using a state-of-the-art high performance liquid chromatography coupled mass spectrometry, to establish the relative regulation of AH proteome in glaucomatous eye and affected cellular pathways, and their correlation with disease pathogenesis
