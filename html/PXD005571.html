Dataset Summary
TitleProteomic analysis of Hepatocellular Adenoma
DescriptionHepatocellular adenomas (HCA) are rare benign tumors that constitute a heterogeneous entity, divided into 5 groups based on molecular features (1) H-HCA with inactivating mutations of Hepatocyte Nuclear Factor 1A (HNF1α), (2) inflammatory HCA (IHCA) with diverse mutations activating the JAK/STAT pathway, (3) b-HCA with activating β-catenin mutation (4) recently identified sh-HCA presenting a Sonic Hedgehog pathway activation and finally, (5) the unclassified HCA (UHCA)). Risk of bleeding of sh-HCA and UHCA is their main clinical feature with malignant transformation. Nowadays, no specific biomarker exists to evaluate this hemorrhage risk. The objective of this study was to combine laser microdissection and mass spectrometry analysis to define Hepatocellular Adenoma proteome in the purpose of identifying specific biomarkers able to predict this bleeding risk.
