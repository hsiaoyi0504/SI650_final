Dataset Summary
TitleMurine kidney glomeruli phosphorylation
DescriptionIsolated murine kidney glomeruli were lysed in buffer containing 8M urea, and proteins were reduced and alkylated. Proteins were digested with trypsin. After reverse-phase chomatrography, peptides were fractionated using strong cation exchange chromatography. Phosphopeptides were enriched using IMAC (FeNTA) columns. Peptides were analyzed by LC-MS/MS on a Q Exactive mass spectrometer or an LTQ Orbitrap XL mass spectrometer. Metadata for Orbitrap samples (MaxQuant Output files include „Orbi“) 1.
