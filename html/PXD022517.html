Dataset Summary
TitleProteomic analysis of early stage infection in the tobacco-Tobacco mosaic virus (TMV)
DescriptionThe early events of virus infection is one of the more poorly understood areas of plant virology and studies on the effect of virus on the host proteome at very early stages of infection are lacking. In the present study, we analysed the proteome on the early stage changes at 15 minutes post inoculation in the tobacco-TMV pathosystem with and without exogenous application of dsRNA p126
