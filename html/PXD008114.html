Dataset Summary
TitleDifferences in proteomic microglia from male and female brains
DescriptionTo determine the contribution at protein levels to the steady state of the cells in different sexes, we estimated the relative amounts of the microglia cells by mass spectrometry-based proteomics approach and further label-free quantification analysis (LFQ). We analysed the differences of protein abundance, comparing the normalized intensity on distinct proteins from four different mice per sex.
