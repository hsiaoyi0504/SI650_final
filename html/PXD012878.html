Dataset Summary
TitleProteomic analysis of dafachronic acid-associated alterations in Haemonchus contortus
DescriptionTo investigate the molecular changes (protein level) associated with dafachronic acid and the nuclear hormone receptor DAF-12 in Haemonchus contortus following exsheathment of the thrid-stage larvae, protein was extracted from replicates (n = 4), processed and analysed using LC-MS/MS.
